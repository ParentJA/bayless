Recipe tequila shooters with spicy chaser

Description

YIELD: about 1 cup sangrita, 4 to 8 shot-size servings

Yield

For serving:

Ingredients

  • ½ pint easy-drinking tequila (see Ingredients in Cook’s Notes)
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges (optional)
  • A small dish of salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. a bottle or pitcher, mix together all the sangrita ingredients. Cover and refrigerate for at least an hour, allowing the flavors to meld.
  2. n you’re ready to serve, pour the sangrita into 4 to 8 tiny glasses (any 1½- to 3-ounce glass can serve the same function as a shot glass), then divide the tequila into 4 to 8 more small glasses. Serve a glass of each to your guests; pass the lime wedges and dish of salt for those who prefer to take their tequila the other way.
